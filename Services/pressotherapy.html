How does it work?

Pressotherapy is a mechanical procedure which is carried out using a specialised equipment targeting specific problem areas. It is a pain-free treatment, designed to enhance the flow of your lymphatic system, improve circulation and enhance deep detoxification. The procedure is similar to a targeted massage performed using powerful air currents and physical waves of various intensity and various pressure.

There are numerous benefits to pressotherapy:

  • Absence of pain or discomfort during the procedure;
  • Elimination of the risk of damaging the skin;
  • Effective cellulitis treatment;
  • Weight loss;
  • Reducing of body oedema through the promotion of lymph drainage;
  • Visible results after a few sessions;
  • Prolonged retention of results of the therapy if appropriate lifestyle changes are implemented;
  • Prevention of varicose veins;
  • Suitability for any client (men or women of any age);
